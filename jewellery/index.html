    THE GOLDEN STORY

    Sangeetha Jewellery, a trusted name since 1986 in Kerala, is famed the world over, for pioneering handcrafted jewellery. Our founder, Mr. EK Paul, envisaged a whole new concept of custom made jewellery in Kerala, promising unsullied quality and original designs. It is his unwavering spirit that has spurred the business to blossom into a brand at par with global standards.

    Sangeetha Jewellery has been a pioneer in the Jewellery industry.  The introduction of 916 gold for the first time in Kerala, in 1997. Popularising the notion of antique jewellery like nagas and temple jewellery in the 90s, certified  diamond jewellery and rose-cut diamonds followed, becoming even greater hit. All these  and more elevated Sangeetha Jewellery to a trendsetter status in elite designer jewellery circles.

    Golden milestones of Sangeetha

     

    Sangeethas unique craftsmanship blends intricate designs with classic tradition and modern styles, creating a whole new statement in design aesthetics.

    2000 Launched certified diamond jewellery
    1986 Inaugurated by Indian film actress Nadhiya Moidu
    1997 Introduced 916 gold in Kerala
    1997 Introduced antique jewellery collection in Kerala

    The Sangeetha Swarnanjali Scheme helps you systematically plan your investment in gold and buy the jewellery youve always wanted to buy.
